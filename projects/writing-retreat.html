Machine Agency Writing Retreat
May 17 & 18 2024, Hawai'i, USA
About the Retreat
Following CHI 2024, members of Machine Agency from the University of Washington will gather for a two day academic writing retreat. Pariticipants will articulate a joint vision for the future of digital fabrication research. Intended outcomes include a draft manuscript to be refined and submitted to a relevent HCI conference venue. Outcomes will be documented on this site following the retreat.
